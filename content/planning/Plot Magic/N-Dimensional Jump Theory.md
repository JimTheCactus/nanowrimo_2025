A new way thinking about [[Layered Dimensional Hyperspace]] that observes that there are a currently unknown number of additional quantized spatial dimensions in addition to the "Hyperspace" dimension that has been known to modern civilization for centuries.

The field was initially pioneered by [[Dr. Ignon]] and the [[Peoples Liberation Army]] researchers, but was further expounded upon by [[Suki Saotomi|Suki]] and later [[Professor Samantha -Sam- Collins|Sam]].
