Rachel settled in to her bunk to rest after the briefing. A new class of cadets would be joining the academy and they would be settling in to their new bunks today. She wasn't responsible for that process, but had been asked to be involved in greeting them at the start-of-term party that had been traditionally been held by the older students at the academy for the incoming freshmen.
